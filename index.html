How to play

Find the hidden equation that produces the target result. Guess a three-digit number, an operator (+ − × ÷), and another three-digit number. The result is computed for you.

  • Right digit, right spot
  • Digit is in that number, wrong spot
  • Digit not in that number

The operator tile turns green when it's correct. Beneath each number a pill shows ▲ Higher or ▼ Lower — telling you which way the hidden number's full value lies (not its digits).

The Remaining possibilities meter shows how many equations still fit everything you've learned. You have 6 guesses.

Two modes

Classic — the result is shown; find the two 3-digit numbers and operator that make it.

Equation — nothing is shown. Guess a whole equation: two 1–2 digit numbers, an operator, and the answer (type the operands, press =, then the result). Your guess must actually balance. Empty leading cells are blanks — a green blank means that number is shorter than its box. Use ↕ to turn the Higher/Lower hints off for a harder game.
